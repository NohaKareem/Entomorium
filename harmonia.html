About Harmonia Axyridis

Harmonia Axyridis, commonly known as a lady bug, or lady beetle is a true paradox. Their beautiful bright coral-red hues marked with dainty polka dots are a mark of danger to ward off potential predactors. Their aphid-heavy diet makes them ideal pest control solutions for farmers, yet they do tend to snack on fruits, such as grapes rendering itself a pest at times! They do also have a tendency to sting, and tend to be a cause for allergies and winter-time home infestations.
