Entomorium is an online field guide offering a rich multimedia encyclopedic experience for entomology enthusiasts and educators.

The name Entomorium is the combination of the words entomology and emporium, indicating a resource hub offering an collection of entomological data for exploration and identification; designed to help spark a curiosity in nature.
